National Identity Card (NIC) Number Calculator

National Identity Card number is unique for each Sri Lankan.
At a glance, it is just a sequence of numbers, but it contains some hidden details such as the person's gender and date of birth.
You can calculate those from the below form. Enter your NIC no and click on the "GO" button.
